Our JOURNEY

See where we came from and how this business came to be

JOURNEY
2013

initial goal

All of us at Kenali Medical wanted to build a company that was more than just a business. We wanted to create an experience for our customers that would inspire them to think of us as a fast, easy, well put together business that provides all their needs when it comes to a moving service.

2014

Business startup

We had our plan, but could we execute it? At first, we had our doubts. After all, it can always be a struggle starting up a business from the ground up. However, after pursuing our dreams and pushing for something truly bigger than we allowed our minds to compare it to, we had built Kenali Medical and we were ready to open ourselves up to the USA!

2015

Developing who we are as a company

We had our company fully operational, but we wanted to really dive into the idea and mindset of who we are as a company. We wanted to be known as one of the best experiences anyone could affiliate with when it comes to moving. So we came up with a business strategy that is being used today to ensure our customer satisfaction is top notch!

2017

Providing a structured and excelling moving experience

We don't want to limit ourselves! We pledge to offer all of our customers a pleasant moving experience and deal with issues as soon as they come up! Thank you for taking the time to read our journey and a HUGE thank you to our customers who made this happen! We couldnt have done it without you!
